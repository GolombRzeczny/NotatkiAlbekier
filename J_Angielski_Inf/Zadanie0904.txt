      Hello, I'm here to help you with sharing your disk in your local network.
      firtsly you have to right click on your c:\ drive in windows explorer, then the contex menu should pop up.
      Click on Properties, new window will open, find sharing tab in this window and click it.
      Now you have to click advance sharing. Check "Share your folder" box and click 'ok' and then close.
      Your drive should be now available in your local network.
